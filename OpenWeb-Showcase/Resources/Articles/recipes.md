There's nothing quite like fresh, homemade pasta. The silky texture and rich flavor simply can't be matched by store-bought varieties. This recipe has been perfected over generations and delivers restaurant-quality results every time.

**Ingredients:**
• 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour or "00" flour
• 3 large eggs, room temperature
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1/2 teaspoon salt

**Instructions:**

1. **Make the dough:** Create a well in the center of your flour on a clean surface. Crack the eggs into the center, add olive oil and salt. Using a fork, gradually incorporate flour from the edges.
